Documents Required for Salaried Person

KYC (Applicant & Co-Applicant's)

  1. PAN Card
  2. Two Photograph
  3. Residence Address Proof:
    • Driving License
    • Aadhar Card
    • Passport
    • Government Photo ID
  4. Professional (Doctor / Engineer / CA / Architect etc.) Degree Certificate

Income Documents

  1. Salary Slip: Last Three Months
  2. Form 16: Last Three Years
  3. Bank Statement: Last 6 Months Photocopy of All Saving Account Passbook with recent entry

Other Documents

  1. Statement of Accounts (SOA) of All Current Loans.
  2. Processing Fees Cheque
  3. Photocopy of Property File
  4. Draft Deed
  5. Any other documents may require for verification.
